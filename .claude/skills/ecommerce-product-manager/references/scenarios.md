# Extended Scenario Examples

## Scenario: International Expansion

**Context**: US-based brand doing $5M annually, 90% US/Canada, considering EU expansion

**Analysis Framework**:

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| Market Size | EU e-commerce: €800B+; UK: £100B+ |
| Platform | Amazon EU (UK, DE, FR, IT, ES) primary; Shopify for D2C |
| Logistics | Pan-EU FBA vs. Multi-Country Inventory (MCI) |
| Compliance | VAT registration, GDPR, product regulations |
| Localization | Translation, pricing in EUR/GBP, local payment methods |

**Implementation Plan**:
1. Month 1-2: VAT registration, trademark verification
2. Month 3-4: Listings translation, pricing analysis
3. Month 5-6: Inventory positioning, launch with Pan-EU FBA
4. Month 7-12: Optimize based on performance data

## Scenario: Subscription Model Transition

**Context**: Consumables brand with 30% monthly repurchase rate, considering subscription offering

**Subscription Design**:

| Element | Decision |
|---------|----------|
| Model | Replenishment (auto-ship same SKU) |
| Frequency | 30, 60, 90-day options |
| Discount | 15% off + free shipping (vs. 10% for one-time) |
| Platform | Recharge (Shopify) or Amazon Subscribe & Save |
| Incentives | First month 50% off, skip/pause flexibility |

**Expected Impact**:
- Subscription customers: 3x LTV vs. one-time
- Predictable revenue: 40% of total within 12 months
- Reduced CAC: retention cheaper than acquisition

## Scenario: Product Line Expansion

**Context**: Single-SKU success ($2M/year), evaluating line extensions

**Decision Matrix**:

| Extension | Synergy | Investment | Risk | Recommendation |
|-----------|---------|------------|------|----------------|
| Color variations | High | Low | Low | ✅ Launch immediately |
| Size variations | High | Medium | Low | ✅ Next priority |
| Complementary product | Medium | Medium | Medium | ⚠️ Test with small batch |
| New category | Low | High | High | ❌ Defer 12+ months |

**Launch Sequence**:
1. Colors (2-3 new): Weeks 1-4
2. Sizes: Weeks 5-12
3. Complementary: Months 4-6 with separate listing test
