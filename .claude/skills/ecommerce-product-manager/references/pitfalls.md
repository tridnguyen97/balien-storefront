# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## Platform-Specific Pitfalls

### Amazon Pitfalls

#### 1. Ignoring the Buy Box
**Symptom**: Listing active but no Buy Box; traffic not converting to sales

**Root Causes**:
- Price not competitive (within 5% of lowest FBA offer)
- Seller metrics below threshold
- New seller without sales history
- FBM competing against FBA offers

**Solution**:
- Use automated repricing tools
- Maintain excellent seller metrics
- Start with FBA to win Buy Box faster
- Monitor Buy Box win rate daily

#### 2. Keyword Stuffing in Titles
**Symptom**: Titles unreadable; low click-through rate; potential policy violation

**Why It Hurts**:
- Amazon's algorithm prioritizes relevance over density
- Customers skip poorly written titles
- Can trigger suppression or account review

**Solution**:
- Write for humans first, algorithms second
- One keyword phrase per title element
- Test readability with fresh eyes

#### 3. Inadequate Inventory Planning
**Symptom**: Frequent stockouts or excessive storage fees

**Impact**:
- Stockout: Ranking drops 50-70%, competitors gain market share
- Overstock: Long-term storage fees, capital tied up

**Solution**:
- Use inventory forecasting formulas
- Maintain 30-60 days cover
- Set automated reorder alerts

### Shopify Pitfalls

#### 1. Theme Bloat
**Symptom**: Slow page load times, poor mobile experience

**Root Causes**:
- Too many apps installed
- Unoptimized images
- Heavy JavaScript
- No CDN usage

**Solution**:
- Audit and remove unused apps
- Compress images (WebP format)
- Use Shopify's CDN
- Test with Google PageSpeed Insights

#### 2. Checkout Friction
**Symptom**: High cart abandonment at checkout step

**Common Issues**:
- Account creation required
- Too many form fields
- No guest checkout
- Hidden fees revealed late

**Solution**:
- Enable guest checkout
- Use Shop Pay for one-click
- Show all costs upfront
- Implement progress indicators

## Strategic Pitfalls

### 1. Single Point of Failure
**Symptom**: 80%+ revenue from single SKU or platform

**Risk**: Platform suspension, competition, or demand shift destroys business

**Mitigation**:
- Diversify across 20+ SKUs by month 6
- Maintain max 60% revenue from any single channel
- Build email list minimum 10% of monthly customers

### 2. Ignoring Unit Economics
**Symptom**: Growing revenue but declining or negative profit

**Warning Signs**:
- CAC increasing faster than AOV
- Promotional discounts deeper than planned
- Return rate eroding margins
- Hidden costs (storage, returns, chargebacks)

**Solution**:
- Calculate true all-in cost per unit
- Set maximum CAC thresholds by channel
- Monitor contribution margin weekly

### 3. Copying Competitors Blindly
**Symptom**: Matching competitor features/pricing without differentiation

**Why It Fails**:
- Commodity positioning
- Race to bottom on price
- No customer loyalty

**Solution**:
- Identify underserved segments
- Build unique value proposition
- Focus on non-price differentiation
